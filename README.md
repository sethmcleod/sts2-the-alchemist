# The Alchemist

> Once a respected apothecary, the decision to abandon their practice came the day they discovered that the most reliable test subject was also the most convenient one. Now a wandering alchemist with a great many notes and very few regrets, they approach the Spire the same way they approach most things in life: with a vial of something inadvisable and the certainty that things are going extraordinarily well. They probably are. It's hard to tell.

The Alchemist has a unique playstyle — deliberately self-poisoning, transforming cards mid-combat, and building a potion economy that feeds back into both offense and defense.

* **Poison, Regen, and Plating as resources.** The Alchemist can apply Poison to themselves, which fuels several attacks and skills that scale with or convert it. Several cards also deal significantly more damage at low HP. Regen and Plating are used to balance the low-HP pressure.
* **Complete The Magnum Opus.** Playing Nigredo produces a chain of four Exhaust tokens, each one enabling the next: converting Poison to Regen, Regen to damage, ending in a burst of Gold, Strength, and a card upgrade.
* **Brew some potions.** The Alchemist can Brew at Rest Sites, which allows you to remove a card from your deck and in exchange you receive a random potion. Potions synergize with offensive and defensive cards, and can also be thrown at the Merchant for Gold.
