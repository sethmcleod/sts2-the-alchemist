# The Alchemist

> Once a respected apothecary, the decision to abandon their practice came the day they discovered that the most reliable test subject was also the most convenient one. Now a wandering alchemist with a great many notes and very few regrets, they approach the Spire the same way they approach most things in life: with a vial of something inadvisable and the certainty that things are going extraordinarily well. They probably are. It's hard to tell.

The Alchemist has a unique playstyle — deliberately self-poisoning, transforming cards mid-combat, and building a potion economy that feeds back into both offense and defense.

* **Poison, Regen, and Plating as resources.** The Alchemist can apply Poison to themselves, which ticks HP down each turn but enables a range of cards that scale with or convert it. Poison can be converted to Regen via Albedo, which in turn heals HP and fuels several attacks and skills. Several cards also deal significantly more damage at low HP.
* **The Magnum Opus.** Playing Nigredo produces a chain of four Exhaust tokens, each one enabling the next: converting Poison to Regen, Regen to damage, and culminating in a burst of Gold, Strength, and card upgrades.
* **Transmutation.** A subset of cards can Transform other cards in your hand or deck into tokens mid-combat — trading a weaker card for something immediately more useful.
* **Potions.** The Alchemist can brew potions at Rest Sites and has several cards that procure or interact with potions during combat. Potions feed directly into offensive and defensive cards, and can also be thrown at the Merchant for Gold.
